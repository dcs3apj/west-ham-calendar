Peterborough United v West Ham United
npower Championship
Saturday 11 February 2012 - 3pm k/o
Latest Ticket Information for the above fixture is as follows:
West Ham United Ticket Office can now confirm that all Standard Match Tickets for the above Peterborough United Away fixture have now SOLD OUT.
 
Remaining seating/terrace tickets went on General Sale & sold out just before close of business on Wednesday 25 January.
 
Please note that 9 Pairs of Wheelchair/Carer Tickets are still available for this fixture.
These tickets can be purchased from West Ham United, at the prices shown, via the below methods.
 
Disabled Ticket Prices are as follows:
8 pairs £25 Adults, £15 Over 60's/Students*, £12 Under 16's (Ambulant / Wheelchair) + complimentary Carer.
10 pairs £20 Adults, £15 Over 60's/Students*, £12 Under 16's (Ambulant / Wheelchair) + complimentary Carer.
 
* In full-time education & in possession of an NUS Card, must be produced on matchday.
 
For those supporters in possession of match tickets, coach travel for this fixture is still available, at the prices shown below.
Please note that the following methods apply when applying for away tickets.
 
Personal Callers - Non-Matchday
Alpari Stand Ticket Office (located next to the Stadium Megastore) between
9am-5pm Monday-Saturdays
 
Personal Callers - Matchday
Alpari Stand Ticket Office (located next to the Stadium Megastore) from
advertised opening times up until kick-off (+ possibly after the match) also
Priory Road Ticket Office (located between the Bobby Moore / East Stand)
2/3 hours before + up to kick-off.
 
Telephone Bookings
0871 222 2700 via Option 3* (9am-5pm Monday-Friday, 9am-1pm Saturdays**)
* Calls cost 10p per minute plus network extras

Postal Bookings
Applications with either Cheques, Postal Orders or Credit Card Payment sent to:
The Ticket Office, PO Box 6731, London, E13 9RA
 
Please note that Cheques/Postal Orders are made payable to
West Ham United FC plc.

Customer Services
 
At West Ham United we want to make sure that all of our supporters receive a fast & efficient service when purchasing match tickets.
 
Great efforts are made to ensure that our supporters receive as high a level of service as is possible when buying tickets either online, via telephone or in person.
 
Should you have a complaint from a recent experience of purchasing tickets or have any comments on the service in general please email ticketoffice@westhamunited.co.uk
 
All genuine complaints and issues will be dealt with by a member of our customer services team who will reply personally within 28 days of being received.
